About Us

Built on a Foundation of Excellence

Sir Phillip Academy was founded with one mission: to give every Nigerian student the preparation, support and confidence they need to excel in their examinations and secure their future.

Under the leadership of Sir Phillip, our academy has guided hundreds of students into top universities, helping them achieve JAMB scores and WAEC grades that open doors to remarkable futures.

We believe every student has the potential to excel. Our expert tutors, structured curriculum, and close-knit community make the difference.
